About the App!

The ATAC web application will assist you in all things management, whether you are managing a team, your personal finances, or your time. Keeping up with the tight schedules of your friends and family has been made easy through the wide range of features offered by ATAC. When your workload is managed on a daily basis, keeping in mind the deadlines and status of each task, more time is spent on being coordinated and maximizing productivity. Later is the best friend of clutter, ATAC now.
